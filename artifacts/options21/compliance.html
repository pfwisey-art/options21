AI & Automation

AI embedded in the workflow
— not bolted on as a chatbot

Every AI feature operates on compliance data the Compliance Officer already owns. PII, AML records, and whistleblower data are explicitly excluded from all AI prompts by design.

Smart Intake
Free-text incident or breach description is converted to structured form fields automatically. Reduces data entry time and improves record consistency.
● LIVE
ASIC Notification Drafter
Breach record converted to a draft s912DAA notification for the Compliance Officer to review and submit. Removes the blank-page problem for a hard 30-day statutory deadline.
● LIVE
Predictive Risk Scoring
30/60/90-day compliance risk forecast using live register data. Outputs a structured risk score with contributing factors identified — not a black-box rating.
● LIVE
AML Typology Alerts
AI-assisted matching against the AUSTRAC typology library — structuring, smurfing, trade-based money laundering, professional money laundering networks. Surfaces SMR recommendation where patterns match.
● LIVE
Regulatory Change Triage
RSS feed items processed by AI and assigned a category, relevance score, and linked obligations. Compliance Officers see only what is relevant to their firm's regulatory footprint.
● LIVE
Automation Engine
10 server-side automation rules: 6 AFSL (breach escalation, CPD expiry, monitoring reminders) and 4 AML (ECDD trigger detection, sanctions escalation, SMR deadline alerts). Runs on schedule without manual intervention.
● LIVE
