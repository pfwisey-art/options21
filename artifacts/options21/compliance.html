What is Veriqua

The compliance platform
AFSL firms actually need

Veriqua is a production multi-tenant SaaS compliance platform purpose-built for Australian AFSL holders and Tranche 2 AUSTRAC-regulated firms. It is the only platform in the Australian market that covers both AFSL/ASIC and AUSTRAC/AML obligations in a single integrated system.

"No competitor offers dual-regime AFSL + AML/CTF in a single platform. Structural moat confirmed."

Built entirely by Paul Wise — the same person who has traded options professionally for 20+ years and runs Options21 — Veriqua is in live daily use by Tenant 1 (Ivanhoe International, AFSL 247412). Every feature has been built to solve a real compliance problem, not a theoretical one.

Who it's for

Built for regulated firms

📜
AFSL Holders

Any firm holding an Australian Financial Services Licence under the Corporations Act. Veriqua covers every register required under s912A, s912DAA, RG 175, RG 271 and RG 181 — in daily live use.

🏦
AUSTRAC-Regulated Firms

Tranche 2 entities — accountants, lawyers, real estate agents and other AUSTRAC-regulated businesses — facing new AML/CTF obligations. Veriqua's AUSTRAC module covers all 15 required program folders.

🔍
Buyers & Investors

Veriqua includes a native Diligence Room — a permission-gated, time-stamped, read-only window with point-in-time Diligence Packs ready for acquirer counsel and external auditors.
