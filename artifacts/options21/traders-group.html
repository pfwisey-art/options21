Common
questions

Do I need to attend live to benefit?+
Not at all. Every session is recorded in full and available within 24 hours. Many members watch replays at their own pace and find it just as valuable. Live attendance is encouraged for Q&A, but the archive is comprehensive.
What experience level is required?+
The Traders Group is best suited to those with at least a basic understanding of what drives financial markets.
What platform is the broadcast on?+
All sessions are broadcast live via GoToMeeting every Thursday at 5:00 PM AWST / 7:00 PM AEST. You'll receive a registration link after joining. GoToMeeting works on desktop, laptop, tablet, and mobile — no special software installation required for browser-based access.
Can I ask questions during the session?+
Yes. Every session includes a dedicated Q&A segment in the final portion. Members submit questions via GoToMeeting's chat function throughout the session and Paul addresses them live.
Is this financial advice?+
No. The Traders Group provides general financial education and market commentary. It does not constitute personal financial advice. Options21 holds AFSL 247412. Please read our Financial Services Guide before making any investment decisions. Always consider your own circumstances and seek personal advice if required.
How do I cancel my membership?+
Memberships can be cancelled at any time with no lock-in. Monthly members retain access until the end of their paid period. Annual members who cancel retain access for the remainder of their annual term. No cancellation fees apply.
