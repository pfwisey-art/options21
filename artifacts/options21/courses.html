Common questions

What experience level do I need?+
The Options Mentoring program is open to all experience levels — Paul begins with solid foundations and builds progressively. The AI Trading Course assumes basic familiarity with trading concepts. The Traders Group is best suited to those with a basic understanding of financial markets.
Are courses delivered live or recorded?+
The Traders Group is delivered live every Thursday and recorded for replay. The Options Mentoring program is delivered live over 3–4 months and recorded. The AI-assisted trading workflow program is a workshop format and is available delivered by Paul personally. All recorded content is accessible for a minimum of 3 years.
What is the refund policy?+
Cloud-based course fees cannot be refunded after your login has been activated. For live courses, full or partial refunds are only considered within the first 2 weeks and require a written request by email. Traders Group refunds are only made in exceptional circumstances — the subscription can be cancelled anytime via email or phone.
Is this personal financial advice?+
No. All Options21 programs provide general financial product advice only under AFSL 247412. Our education does not take into account your personal objectives, financial situation or needs. We strongly recommend seeking independent financial advice before making any investment decisions.
How do I pay?+
Course fees are payable by credit card or direct bank transfer before the program begins. The Traders Group monthly subscription is payable by credit card or direct deposit on the first day of each month. Contact us for bank transfer details.
