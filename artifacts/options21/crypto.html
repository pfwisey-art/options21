AFSL 247412 · Regulated Advice

What an AFSL holder
can advise on

Under Australian Financial Services Licence 247412 (Ivanhoe International Pty Ltd), Paul Wise is authorised to provide general financial product advice on crypto-related instruments that are classified as financial products under the Corporations Act 2001. This includes ASX and CBOE-listed crypto ETFs and ETPs, and derivative products approved by ASIC.

✅
Within AFSL Scope
  • ASX & CBOE-listed Bitcoin and Ethereum ETFs
  • US-listed crypto ETFs (via licensed broker)
  • ASIC-approved crypto derivatives and structured products
  • Listed equities with crypto exposure (MARA, MSTR, Coinbase)
  • General analysis of Bitcoin and digital asset market cycles
🏛️
Regulatory Framework
  • ASIC — approves crypto financial products, ETFs and derivatives for Australian markets
  • AUSTRAC — registers Digital Currency Exchanges for AML/CTF compliance
  • Corporations Act 2001 — determines whether a crypto asset is a financial product
⚠️
Outside AFSL Scope
  • Direct spot crypto purchases on DCEs (not classified as a financial product)
  • DeFi protocols, staking, and yield products
  • NFTs and non-financial digital assets
  • Personal financial advice (requires a Statement of Advice)

Crypto derivatives — ASIC & AUSTRAC framework

Where a derivative product over a crypto asset has been approved by ASIC as a financial product, Paul Wise is authorised under AFSL 247412 to provide general advice on that instrument.
