Privacy Policy

How Ivanhoe International Pty Ltd collects, uses and protects your personal information.

Ivanhoe International Pty Ltd (ABN 69 076 144 557) · Trading as Options21 · AFSL 247412 · Last updated May 2026

This Privacy Statement explains how Ivanhoe International Pty Ltd (ABN 69 076 144 557), trading as "Options21" ("we, us, our") collects personal information about you and maintains, uses and discloses that information.

Your personal information will be treated strictly in accordance with the National Privacy Principles in the Australian Privacy Act.

Gathering of Information

We do not collect your personally identifying information, unless you knowingly provide that information. For example, when you ask for information about our products, or you complete an online request or order form for our products, or you register on our website to receive a newsletter. Only the information which you type into the relevant online forms is collected by us.

Payment Information

Our websites do not offer online payments. Payment information taken over the phone is used for single transactions only and then discarded, unless you have entered into a monthly subscription, in which case your card information will be securely stored and re-used until the cancellation of your subscription.

Use of Information

We collect your information for use by our business only. We do not sell or otherwise disclose your information to any third parties. Our primary goal in collecting information from you is to be able to notify you of products or services you might be interested in in future. Every such notice is delivered via email and will contain a link titled "opt-out" or "no more emails please…" at the bottom of it. The effect of requesting removal from our mailing list is instant.
