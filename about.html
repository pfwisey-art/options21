Background

The approach

Paul Wise has been trading options with real capital for over two decades. His approach is research-first and evidence-based — he does not teach theoretical strategies but the exact frameworks he applies to his own live trading every week.

"The edge is never in the tool. It's in understanding what drives volatility before the market prices it in."

When trading, Paul focuses on three primary areas: mean reversion of volatility, strategies based on the US earnings season, and inverse volatility ETNs. He has developed a high level of competency in these areas and shares his live analysis with Traders Group members every Thursday.

Beyond trading, Paul is the sole developer of Veriqua — a production-grade SaaS compliance platform purpose-built for Australian AFSL holders and AUSTRAC-regulated firms. His unique combination of market depth and regulatory understanding sets Options21 apart from any other Australian options education provider.

Options21 operates under AFSL 247412, issued by the Australian Securities and Investments Commission (ASIC). Paul provides general financial advice only and does not provide personal financial advice. All clients are encouraged to consider their own financial circumstances and seek independent advice as appropriate.

Who Are We?

Ivanhoe International Pty Ltd began operations in October 1996 and started trading as Options21 in February 2001. Options21 is also a distributor of Optuma technical analysis software, produced by Market Analyst International Pty Ltd.

How Are We Paid?

Course fees: Between $2,995 and $5,987 depending on the course. Payable before the course starts via credit card or direct bank transfer. Cloud-hosted courses are accessible within one day of payment and remain accessible for at least 3 years.

Traders Group subscription: A weekly online webcast available by monthly subscription, payable on the first day of every month. Can be cancelled at any time via email or phone on the last business day of the preceding month.

Software products: We distribute Optuma software. Payment is taken directly by Optuma. Options21 receives a small commission per sale.

Employee remuneration: Employees do not receive specific payments or commissions for financial services provided. Employees and directors are remunerated by salary and discretionary bonus.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Cloud-based course fees cannot be refunded after the login has been activated. Full or partial refunds for live online courses will only be considered within the first 2 weeks and require a written reason by email. Traders Group refunds are only made in exceptional circumstances — the service can be cancelled at any time, but cancellations must be received on or before the last business day of the previous month to stop future payments.

We do not have any relationships or associations which might influence us in providing you with our services.
